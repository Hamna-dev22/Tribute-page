Tribute to Arfa Karim

Born: February 2, 1995

Died: January 14, 2012

Arfa Karim Randhawa was a brilliant Pakistani computer prodigy who made history by becoming the youngest Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP) at the age of nine. Her unmatched intellect and passion for technology inspired millions.

She represented Pakistan at global forums, including a memorable visit to Microsoft headquarters where she met Bill Gates. Arfa's achievements earned her the President's Pride of Performance Award, making her one of the youngest recipients of the honor.

Tragically, Arfa passed away at the young age of 16, but her legacy lives on as a symbol of hope and inspiration for countless individuals worldwide.
